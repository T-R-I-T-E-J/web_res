National Competitions

The NRAI organizes several national-level competitions throughout the year, including the National Shooting Championship Competitions (NSCC), All India G.V. Mavlankar Shooting Championship, and various Zonal Championships.

About NRAI

The National Rifle Association of India (NRAI) was founded in 1951 with the view to promote and popularize the shooting sports in India.
